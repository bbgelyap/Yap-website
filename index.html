BIOGRAPHY

Early Life & Beginnings

Born Ashton Simmonds on April 5, 1995, in Oshawa, Ontario, Daniel Caesar grew up in a religious household and began singing in church. After leaving home as a teenager to pursue music, he released early EPs like Praise Break (2014), building a loyal fanbase through raw, heartfelt songwriting.

Breakthrough & Acclaim

With his debut album Freudian (2017), Daniel achieved global recognition. The album earned him a Grammy Award for Best R&B Performance for 'Best Part' (featuring H.E.R.), and tracks like 'Get You' and 'Blessed' solidified his place as a leading voice in modern R&B.

Artistic Evolution

His follow-up albums Case Study 01 (2019) and Never Enough (2023) showcased growth in sound and themes of self-discovery. He continues to collaborate with artists across genres and use his platform to advocate for social justice and mental health awareness.
